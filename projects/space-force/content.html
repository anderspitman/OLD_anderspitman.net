Introduction

SpaceForce is a simple space shooter game, where you colonize planets and try to take over the whole map. It is heavily inspired by Gravity Well 3.x, which was a game I randomly had a copy of as a kid in the 90s. I consider this a Gravity Well clone. A demo of my current progress can be found here.

Goals

  • Bring back the super old-school feel of version 3.x (newer versions have improved the graphics somewhat)
  • Make the game easily accessible on a variety of platforms (ie runs in a browser)
  • Open source. Code is currently here
  • Match the visual style and most of the gameplay mechanics of Gravity Well
  • Add multiplayer support
  • I'm hoping to eventually extract a game engine from this project. It would be a simple engine designed for making vector-graphics games. Currently it renders everything to SVG and I'm going to avoid adding other backends if I can. At the very least, I think I'll come away from this with a simple 2D drawing library. I'm planning to call that "vektar", which is why the repo is currently named as such.

Not [currently] goals:

  • Adding player AI (I am planning on implementing shuttle and defense AI)
